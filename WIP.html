    Why did I create this portfolio?

    As a finance professional, I have used several techniques to bring, manipulate, analyse and report data on Microsoft Excel. All those techniques accomplished the task at hand however, they were not the most efficient way of completing activities as they would normally include a considerable amount of repetition. Some of the techniques I used include VBA Excel (macro programming) and SQL queries against CSV files to obtain and transform data. The rise of cloud computing and machine learning is reshaping all industries and finance (accounting especifically) is not an exception. This trend has forced me to reconsider the approach I was taking towards technological change and its effect on how efficient the practices I follow at work were.

    I have created this small portfolio to hlep me leave spreadsheets behind by learning programming languages to prepare, analyse and report financial information and to automate tasks to bring efficiencies to my workplace.
